## What is MapTales?

MapTales turns scattered trips into one story worth keeping. Instead of losing
your travels in a camera roll, you drop pins as you go and MapTales stitches them
into a living map, an organized timeline, and a recap you'll actually want to
share.

## ✨ What's inside

| | Feature | What it does |
|---|---|---|
| 📍 | **One living map** | Every stop drops a pin, every journey adds a line. Your whole travel history, always growing. |
| 🏅 | **Milestones** | Unlock badges as you go — first solo trip, five countries, café hopper. |
| 🛂 | **Passport** | Cities, places and stories stamped and organized by country. |
| 🎞️ | **Wrapped** | At year's end, MapTales wraps your travels into a cinematic, shareable recap. |

## 📱 The app

Three tabs, one story:

- **Map** — the living map of everywhere you've been
- **Journeys** — every trip folded into its own timeline
- **You** — passport, milestones, and your yearly Wrapped
